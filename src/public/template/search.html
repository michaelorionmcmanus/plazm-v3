  • About

    About Plazm.org publishes both new work and extensive archives related to art, design, and culture. Acting as an online library, the site features international contributors in addition to contextualizing Portland's creative culture from 1990 to the present. The site is a presentation of the nonprofit organization New Oregon Arts & Letters. Tiffany Lee Brown is the editor. Plazm.org was designed by Joshua Berger and engineered by the fabulous Michael McManus. We are forever grateful to Oregon Cultural Trust for a grant helping us launch this project.
